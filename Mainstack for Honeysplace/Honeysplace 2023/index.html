About Us

Honeysbeauty children's place is one of the leading online children's stores for kids wears from 12 months to 12 years. We are located in nigeria but serve all countries across the world. We carry a wide range of baby clothes, toddler girl dresses, baby girl leggings, baby shoes, toddler boots, kids fashion, kids shirts, boys shirt and girls skirts.
